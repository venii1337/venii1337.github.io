About Us

Global Alliance Advisory Company Limited (“Global Alliance Co. Ltd.”) engages in Investment Banking and Financial Advisory Services.

Global Alliance is capable of cooperating with top levels of management & executives of multinational enterprises, listed companies in the Stock Exchange of Thailand, large scale corporations, financial institutions, underwriters, lenders, investors, professional service providers, regulators, ultimate shareholders and beneficial owners.

Our clients and linkages in the Stock Exchange of Thailand along with our partners across almost all industries within the Thai Economy allows us to utilize an extensive network locally and abroad.

Our head team has wide-ranging decades of experience in investment banking, capital market and corporate finance with the background from various financial institutions, brokerage companies and boutique advisory houses.

Our key advantage is our flexibility to serve the demands of our customers and clients with our linkages in the Stock Exchange of Thailand along with our partners across almost all industries within the Thai Economy. This allows us to utilize an extensive network locally and abroad.

Our goal is to satisfy the wishes of our potential clients and further expand our network. Connecting the Kingdom with other international business hubs.
